About Me

I’m José Antonio Torres, a multidisciplinary data professional with a background in industrial engineering and a passion for transforming data into meaningful business insights. I’ve led analytics projects in sales, risk, and operations—leveraging Python, Tableau, SQL, and Alteryx to drive agile decision-making.
